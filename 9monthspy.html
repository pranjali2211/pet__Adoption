SPHYNX CAT

Congratulations on adopting a 8-month-old Tiger cat 🐱!

✅1.Home Setup🏠

    Because Sphynx cats don't have fur:

  • Keep the house Warm(20-25c)
  • Provide soft blankrts or warm beds
  • Avoid cold floors or strong AC
  • Let them sleep in cozy spots
    • They love being close to people

✅2.Feeding🍽️

    Sphynx cats have fast metabolism, so they eat a little more than other cats.

    Feeds 2-3 meals daily:
  • High-quality kitten or young-cat food
  • Mix of wet+dry food
    • Always keep fresh water available. Avoid:
  • Chocolate
  • Onion/garlic
  • Spicy or oily human food

✅3.Bathing 🛁( Very Important)

    Unlike furry cats, Sphynx need regular bath

  • Routine:
  • Bath once every 1-2 weeks
  • Use mild cat shampoo
  • Gently clean skin folds

✅4.Ear👂 & Skin Care

    Sphynx produce more oil:

    Weekly care:

  • Clean ears with cotton pad
  • Wips skin Gently if oily
  • Trim nails every 3-4 weeks

✅5.Play & Activity🏃

    Sphynx cats are very energetic and social.

  • Daily:
  • 20-30 minutes play time
  • Feather wand, toys, balls or tunnels
  • Climbing or cat tree

✅6.🩺Health Care

    Visit a vet for:

  • vaccinations
  • Deworming
  • Flea prevention
  • Spaying/neutering (if not done)

    • Watch for:

  • Skin Irritation
  • Old sensitivity

✅Bonding🤍

    Sphynx cats are known as "velcro cats" because they stick to their owners.

  • Speak softly
  • Spend time cudding
  • Talk to them
  • Let them sit on your lap

    • They are Very loving and friendly
