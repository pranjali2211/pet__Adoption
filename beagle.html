BEAGLE DOG

🐾Congrats on adopting your 7-month-old Beagle!🐾🐶

✅1.Vet & Health🩺 (Very Important)

    Take your puppy to a vet within the first week

  • Ask about:
  • Vaccinations (rabies, DHPP)
  • Deworming & flea/tick prevention
  • Microchipping

✅2.First few days (very important)

  • Give time to settle: Your puppy may be shy, excited, or confused. That’s normal.
  • Create a routine: Same times every day for:
  • Meals
  • Walks / potty
  • Play
  • Sleep
  • Safe space: Give them a quiet bed or crate where they can relax.

✅3.Food & water

  • Feedpuppy food (not adult food).
  • Usually3 meals a day at 4 months.
  • Fresh water available all the time.
  • Don’t give human food—Beagles gain weight easily.

✅4.Toilet training (Beagles need patience!)

  • Take them outside every 2–3 hours
  • Always go:
  • After waking up
  • After eating
  • After playing
  • Praise immediately when they go outside 🚀
  • Never punish accidents—just clean and try again.

✅5.Training (Start Now!)

Beagles are smart but stubborn 😄

  • Teach basic commands:
  • Sit
  • Come
  • Stay
  • Usetreats + praise , not shouting
  • Keep training sessions short (5–10 minutes)

✅6. Socialization

  • Let your puppy meet:
  • Friendly people
  • Other vaccinated dogs
  • This helps prevent fear and bad behavior later

✅7.🏃Exercise & Play🏃

  • Beagles have lots of energy
  • Daily walks + playtime
  • Safe toys for chewing (they love to chew!)

✅8.Safety at Home

  • Remove small objects they can swallow
  • Keep shoes, wires, and trash away

✅9.Love & Patience ❤️

  • Your puppy may feel scared or confused at first
  • Give time, routine, and affection
  • Beagles bond strongly with their family
