BULLDOG

🐾Congratulations on adopting a 1-year-old 🐶BullDog🐾!

✅1.🩺Health first🩺 (very important for Bulldogs)

  • Vet check if not done recently
  • Ask about:
  • Breathing issues (very common)
  • Skin infections & wrinkles
  • Weight (bulldogs gain weight easily)
  • Keep vaccinations, deworming, flea control up to date
    • ⚠️ Bulldogs overheat easily
  • Never exercise in hot weather
  • Always provide shade & fresh water
  • Heavy panting = stop activity immediately

✅2.Food & diet

  • High-quality adult dog food
  • Usually 2 meals per day
  • Avoid:
  • Fatty foods
  • Too many treats
  • Measure food (obesity causes serious health problems)
    • 💡 Ask your vet about food for:
  • Sensitive stomach
  • Skin & allergies

✅3.🏃Exercise (short but regular)

Bulldogs are low-energy, not lazy:

  • 20–30 minutes total per day
  • Short walks
  • Light play indoors
  • NO long runs or stairs

✅4.Training & behavior

At 1 year, bulldogs can be stubborn but smart:

  • Use positive training only
  • Short sessions (5–10 minutes)
  • Focus on:
  • Sit
  • Stay
  • Come
  • Leash walking

    • ❌ Don’t yell or punish — they shut down

✅5.Skin & wrinkle care (VERY IMPORTANT)

  • Clean face wrinkles daily or every other day
  • Use a soft cloth + dog-safe wipes
  • Dry completely (moisture causes infection)
  • Check:
  • Ears
  • Tail pocket (if present)
  • Paws

✅6.🧼Grooming🧼

  • Brush 1–2 times a week
  • Bathe every 4–6 weeks (or as needed)
  • Trim nails monthly
  • Clean ears regularly

✅7.Sleep & comfort

  • Soft bed (joint support helps)
  • Cool room
  • Bulldogs snore — this is normal 😄

✅8.Personality tips

  • Very loving & loyal
  • Can be stubborn
  • Loves routine
