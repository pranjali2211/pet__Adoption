COCKATIEL BIRD

Congratulations on adopting a 1-year-old Cockatiel!

✅1.🏠Cage Setup🐦

    Cockatiel need space to move and stretch their wings.
    Recommended Setup:

  • Large cage (at least~60*60*60cm or bigger)
  • 2-3 natural perches
  • 2-3 perches of different sizes
  • Food & water bowls
  • Toys (bells,swings,chew toys)
    • Place the Cage:
  • in a bright room with natural light
  • Away from kitchen smoke, strong perfume, and drafts.

✅2.Diet🥗

    A balanced diet is important.

  • Daily Food :
  • Cockatiel pellets or seed mix
  • Fresh vegetables (spinach, carrot, broccoli)
  • Small fruit pices:apple,banana(occasionally)
    • Also provide :
    Fresh water daily
    Cuttlebonw or mineral block for calcium
    Avoid :
  • Chocolate
  • Onion/garlic
  • Salty or sugary food
  • Avocado

✅3.Exersice & Play🕊️

    Cockaties are playful birds.

    Daily Routine:
  • 30-60 minutes outside the cage (supervised)
  • Provide toys to chew and explore
  • Rotate toys weekly to prevent boredom

✅4.Cage Cleaning🧹

    Clean environment is keep birds healthy.

  • Routine:
  • Change cage paper every day or 2
  • Wash food and water bowls daily
  • Deep clean cage once a week

✅5.🩺Health Care

    Watch for signs of illness:

  • Fluffed feathers constantly
  • loss of appetite
  • Sitting quietly all day
  • Changes in droppings
    • if you see these signs,consult anavian veterinarian.

✅6.Bonding🤝

    Cockatiels are very affectionate.

    To build trust:
  • Talk softly to the bird
  • Offer treats on your hand
  • Let it sit on your finger or shoulder
  • Spend time daily near the cage
    • They offer enjoy whistling and interacting with people.

✅7.Sleep🌙

    Cockatiels needs 10-12 hours of sleep.

    Tips:
  • keep cage in a quiet area at night
  • Cover the cage lightly if needed.
