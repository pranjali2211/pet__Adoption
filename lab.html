LABRADOR DOG

🐾Congratulations on adopting a 2-month-old Labrador🐶 puppy!🐾

✅1. First Week After Adoption

Vet Visit🩺

Take your puppy to a veterinarian for:

  • Health check-up
  • Vaccination schedule
  • Deworming plan
  • Flea/tick prevention
    • Very important at this age.

✅2. Feeding

  • What to feed:
  • High-quality puppy dog food (starter/puppy formula)
  • Do NOT give milk; many puppies cannot digest it.
  • Feeding schedule:
  • 3–4 meals per day
  • Provide clean water at all times
  • Approximate meal timings:
  • Morning: 8 AM
  • Noon: 1 PM
  • Evening: 6 PM
  • Night (optional small meal): 9–10 PM

✅3. Training & Behavior

  • Start toilet training (take the puppy outside after meals, naps, play)
  • Teach simple commands: sit, no, come
  • Puppies bite while teething—give chew toys

✅4. Socialization

Expose your puppy slowly to:
  • People
  • Sounds
  • Gentle pets
  • New environments
    • (But avoid dog parks until vaccines are complete.)

✅5.🏃Exercise🏃

A 2-month-old needs light play only:
  • 10–15 minutes of gentle play 3–4 times/day
  • No long walks (bones are still soft)

✅6.🧼Grooming🧼

  • Brush 2–3 times/week
  • Weekly bath (mild puppy shampoo)
  • Clean ears often (Labradors get infections quickly)
  • Trim nails every 2–3 weeks
