GOLDEN RETRIEVER

🐾Congratulations on adopting a 7-month-old Golden Retriever Dog!🐶🐾

✅1.Vet visit 🩺(ASAP)

  • Full health check
  • Make sure vaccinations are up to date
  • Ask about deworming, flea/tick prevention
  • Microchip check (or get one)

✅2.Food & feeding🥣

  • Feed puppy food for large breeds
  • Usually 2–3 meals per day
  • No human food, chocolate, grapes, onions, etc.

✅3.Training 🎯(start now)

At 7 months, training is VERY important:
  • Teach basics: sit, stay, come, down
  • Use positive reinforcement (treats + praise)
  • Be consistent—same rules every day

✅4. Socialization

  • Safely meet other dogs and people
  • Expose to sounds, cars, grooming, vet handling
  • This helps prevent fear and aggression later

✅5.🏃Exercise🏃

Retrievers have lots of energy:
  • 1–2 hours daily (walks + play)
  • Games like fetch are great
  • Don’t overdo jumping (joints still growing)

✅6.Chewing & teething

  • Provide safe chew toys
  • Hide shoes and valuables 😄
  • Redirect chewing, don’t punish
    • At home 🏡setup
  • Comfortable bed
  • Food & water bowls
  • Collar + leash
  • ID tag with contact info
  • Crate (optional but helpful for training)
