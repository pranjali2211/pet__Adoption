MINI REX RABBIT

🐇Congratulations on adopting a 3-month-old Mini Rex rabbit 🐰!

✅ 1.First day setup🏡

    Must-have items:
  • cage or hutch(at least 3x2 feet for one rabbit)
  • water bottle+bowl
  • hay feeder
  • litter box:
  • Add hay in or next to the box—rabbits like to eat while they go
  • soft badding (paper-based not cedar/pine)
  • toys(chew toys,tunnels,balls)
  • hiding place (small box or hideout)
  • keep the rabbitin a quiet room at first
  • limit access to wire,toxic plants,and small objects
  • rabbit need supervised free-room time outside the cage

✅ 2.🥕🌾 Diet (Very Important at This Age)🥬🌿

    Staple:
  • unlimited timothy hay(for digestion and teeth)
    • 🟤Pellets: 1/8-1/4 cup daily of high-quality rabbit pallets
  • Fresh vaggies (introduce slowly)
  • Romaine lettuce and cabbage (can cause gas)
    • Treats: small fruit pieces occasionally (apple,strawberry)
  • never chocolate,candy,or human
  • Romaine lettuce
  • Water
  • fresh water at all time

✅ 3. Grooming care🧼

  • Coat: Mini Rex have short, plush fur
  • Brush 1×/week, more during shedding
  • Nails: Trim every 4–6 weeks
  • Baths: ❌ Never bathe a rabbit (spot clean only)

✅ 4.❤️ Handling & Bondinge

  • Rabbits are prey animals—go slow.
  • Sit on the floor and let them come to you.
  • Always support the back legs when lifting.
  • Signs of happiness:
  • Flopping onto their side
  • Tooth purring (gentle grinding)
  • Zoomies & binkies

✅ 5.🩺Health & Vet Care🐇

  • Find an exotic vet
    • Regular dog/cat vets usuallydon’t treat rabbits ✂️ Spay/Neuter (very important)
  • Around 4–6 months
  • Benefits:
  • Prevents cancer
  • Reduces aggression
  • Makes litter training easier
