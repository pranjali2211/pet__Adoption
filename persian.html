PERSIAN CAT

Congratulations on adopting a 6-month-old Persian Cat🐱!

✅ 1. First Day Setup Must-have items

  • Soft bed or blanket
  • Litter box + clumping litter
  • Food & water bowls (wide, shallow)
  • Kitten food (dry + wet)
  • Scratching post
  • Comb + metal grooming brush
  • Carrier
  • Toys (feather wand, small balls)
  • Safe room setup
  • Keep the cat in one room for the first 1–2 days
  • Provide hiding spots(box, blanket)
  • Avoid loud noises
  • Let the cat explore slowly

✅ 2. Food & Feeding Routine At 6 months:

  • Feed 3 times a day
  • Provide kitten food (high protein for growth)
  • Diet example:
  • Morning: 1–2 tbsp wet food
  • Afternoon: small amount of dry food
  • Night: wet or dry food
  • Fresh water always available
  • Avoid:
  • Milk
  • Human food (spices, sweets, onion, garlic)

✅ 3. Litter Training

  • Persians are usually clean, but:
  • Keep litter box in a quiet corner
  • Clean daily
  • Use unscented clumping litter
  • If accidents happen, don’t scold—just clean the spot

✅ 4. Grooming 🧼(VERY IMPORTANT for Persians)🧼

  • Persian cats have long fur that mats easily.
  • Daily grooming (5–10 mins):
  • Comb gently
  • Remove small knots before they get worse
  • Weekly:
  • Eye cleaning (Persians tear a lot; use soft wet cotton)
  • Nail trimming
  • Optional:
  • Monthly bath using cat shampoo

✅ 5. 🩺Health & Vet Care🩺

  • Ask the previous owner/rescue:
  • Vaccination status
  • Deworming dates
  • Any past illness
  • You must do:
  • Complete vaccinations
  • Deworm every 3 months
  • Check for fleas
  • Discuss spaying/neutering (ideal age: 6–8 months)

✅ 6. Behaviour & Training At this age:

  • They are playful but also calm
  • They may hide for 1–3 days—normal
  • Give space but offer gentle interaction
  • Train simple things:
  • Litter habits
  • Scratching post
  • Not biting during play

✅ 7. Environment & Comfort Persians prefer:

  • Cool rooms
  • Clean floors
  • Low noise
  • Soft beds
  • Keep them indoors—they are delicate and slow runners.

✅ 8.❤️ Bonding Tip To build trust:

  • Sit near them quietly
  • Offer treats
  • Use soft voice
  • Play 10–15 minutes daily
  • Don’t force cuddling
  • Within a week, they usually start following you around.
