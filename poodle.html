POODLE DOG

🐾Congrats on adopting your 6-month-old poodle!🐾🐶

✅1.First week at Home

    Give the puppy a quiet space to adjust
  • Provide:
  • comfortable bed
  • food and water bowls
  • puppy toys
  • Tranining pads or outdoor potty area
  • Avoid too many visitors in the first few days.

✅2.Feeding

  • Morning: puppy dry food
  • Afternoon: small portion or snack
  • Evening: dry food or wet puppy food
    • Tips:
  • Use high-quality puppy food
  • Always Keep Fresh water available
  • Avoid chocolate,onions,grapes,spicy food.

✅3.🏃Exercise

    Poodle have high energy.
  • 2 walks(20-30 minutes each)
  • play time with toys or ball
  • Fresh water available all the time.
  • Mental games ( poodles are very smart)

✅4.Training

  • Sit
  • Stay
  • Come
  • Leash walking
  • Use positive rewards (treats or praise)

✅5.Grooming

    poodles need regular grooming
  • Routine:
  • Brush 3-4 times per week
  • BAth every 3-4 weeks
  • Hair trimming every 6-8 weeks
  • Clean ears weekly

✅6.Health🩺Care

  • ckeck with a vet for:
  • vaccinations
  • Deworming
  • Flea and tick preventioon
  • Spaying neutering (around this age)

✅7.Bonding❤️

  • Spend time playing every day
  • Talk gentaly to the puppy
  • Reward good behavior
    • Poodles ar every loyal and love attention
