SIAMESE CAT

Congratulations on adopting a 5-month-old Siamese cat 🐱!

✅1.🩺Vet care🩺 (important now)

  • Vet check if not done yet
  • Vaccinations (usually still finishing kitten shots)
  • Deworming & flea prevention
  • Ask about spay/neuter (often done around 5–6 months)

✅2.Food & feeding

  • Feed kitten food (high protein)
  • 3 small meals per day (or free-feed if vet approves)
  • Fresh water always available
    • ❌ Avoid:
  • Cow’s milk
  • Human food
  • Fish as a daily meal

✅3.Litter box habits

  • 1 litter box (quiet location)
  • Scoop daily
  • Clean weekly
  • Use unscented litter (Siamese can be picky)

✅4.Play & exercise 🏃(VERY important)

    Siamese have high energy and intelligence:

  • Play 2–3 times a day (10–15 minutes each)
  • Best toys:
  • Wand toys
  • Balls
  • Puzzle toys

    • 💡 Bored Siamese = loud meowing & trouble

✅5.Training & behavior

Yes, you can train a cat!

  • Teach:
  • Name recognition
  • “Come”
  • Simple tricks
  • Use treats & praise
  • Never punish

✅6.Social needs (this breed needs YOU)

  • Siamese hate being alone
  • Talk to them—they respond!
  • If left alone a lot, consider:
  • Another cat
  • Interactive toys

✅7.🧼Grooming🧼

  • Short coat = easy care
  • Brush once a week
  • Trim nails every 2–3 weeks
  • Clean ears if dirty

✅8.Safety

  • Keep indoors (recommended)
  • Remove:
  • Toxic plants
  • Loose strings
  • Small swallowable items
  • Secure windows & balconies

✅9.What’s normal for Siamese

  • Loud meowing (normal 😸)
  • Very attached to owners
  • Follows you everywhere
  • Curious and playful
