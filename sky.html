PARAKEET

Congratulations on adopting a 1-year-old Parakeet!

✅1.Cage Setup🦜

    Give the bird a Comfortable and safe Home.
    Recommended Setup:

  • Give the cat a quiet room to settle
  • Medium or large Cage of different sizes
  • 2-3 perches of different sizes
  • Food & water bowls
  • A few toys ()bells,swings,chew toys
    • Tips:
  • Place the cage in a bright but clean area
  • Avoid kitchen smoke, strong perfume, and direct fan/AC wind.

✅2.Diet🥗

    A balanced diet keeps your Parakeet healthy.

  • Daily Food :
  • Parakeet seeds mix or pellets
  • Fresh vegetables (spinach, carrot, broccoli)
  • Small fruit pices occasionally(apple,banana)
    • Always provide :
    Fresh water daily
    Avoid :
  • Chocolate
  • Onion/garlic
  • Salty snacks

✅3.Exersice & Activity🕊️

    parakkets are very active birds.

    Daily Routine:
  • Let the bird have supervised flying time outside the cage
  • Provide toys to prevent boredom
  • Rotate toys every few weeks
    • Exercise keep them mentally healthy

✅4.Cage Cleaning🧹

    Clean environment is important.

  • Routine:
  • Change cage paper daily or every 2 days
  • Wash food and water bowls daily
  • Deep clean cage once a week

✅5.🩺Health Care

    Watch for signs of illness:

  • Fluffed feathers all the time
  • Not eating
  • Sitting quietly for long periods
  • Changes in droppings
    • if you see these signs,visit a bird veterinarian.

✅6.Bonding With Your Bird🤝

    At 1 year, parakeets can still become very friendly.

    Bonding tips:
  • Speak softly near the cage
  • Offer treats on your hand
  • Spend time daily near the bird
  • Be patient-trust builds slowly

✅7.Sleep🛏️

    Parakeet needs 10-12 hours of sleep.

    Tips:
  • keep cage in a quiet area at night
  • Cover the cage lightly with cloth if needed.
