SPHYNX CAT

Congratulations on adopting a 9-month-old sphynx cat🐱!

✅1.Prepare Your Home

    Sphynx cats lack fur ,so they get cold easily. What to set up:
  • 🛏️ Warm beds or blankets
  • ☀️ Sunny resting spots
  • 👕 Optional cat sweaters in cooler weather
  • 🧸 Toys and climbing spaces
    • Keep the room temperature around 22–26 °C if possible.

✅2.Food & feeding

    Sphynx cats have a faster metabolism to maintain body heat. Diet tips:>
  • High-quality high-protein cat food
  • Feed 3 small meals per day at this age
  • Fresh water always available
    • Avoid:
  • Chocolate
  • Onion/garlic
  • Cow milk (can upset stomach)

✅3.Litter Training

    Most 9-month Sphynx cats are already trained. Tips:
  • keep the litter box very clean (they prefer cleanliness)
  • place it quite corner
  • use unscented litter

✅4.Skin Care (Very Important)

    Unlike furry cats, Sphynx produce skin oils that don’t get absorbed by fur. Routine:
  • 🛁 Bath once every 1–2 weeks
  • Use gentle cat shampoo
  • Clean skin folds carefully
  • Dry completely to prevent chills
    • Also:
  • Wipe skin with a damp cloth if it gets oily.

    • Ear Cleaning:

    Clean ears weekly

    Nail & Paw Care:

    Trim nails every 2–3 weeks

✅5.Social Needs

    Sphynx are extremely people-loving ❤️ They enjoy:
  • Being carried
  • Sleeping with owners
  • Interactive play
    • If left alone too long, they may become lonely or anxious.

✅6.Health Care

    Common checks:
  • Vaccinations
  • Parasite prevention
  • Heart screening for Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy , which can occur in this breed.

✅7.Sun & Temperature Safety

    Sphynx cats love body heat. Expect your cat to sleep:
  • under blankets
  • on your lap
  • near your neck or chest 😄
