TIGER CAT

Congratulations on adopting a 8-month-old Tiger cat 🐱!

✅1.First Few Days at Home

  • Give the cat a quiet room to settle
  • Provide:
  • Litter box
  • Food & water bowls
  • Comfortable bed or blanket
  • Scratching post
  • A few toys

✅2.Food & feeding

    At this age feed 2-3 meals per day

  • Good Options:
  • Quality kitten food (still needed until about 1 year)
  • Wet food once a day
  • Dry food for other meals
    • Always provide fresh water
    Avoid: Chocolate,Onion/garlic,Spicy human food,Too much milk

✅3.Litter Training

    Most cats learn quickly:
    Tips:
  • keep the litter box clean dailt
  • place it in a quite corner
  • use unscented litter

✅4.Play & exercise 🏃

    Tiger/tabby cats are very energetic

  • Daily play:
  • 15-30 minutes play sessions
  • Feather toys or balls
  • Climbing or Scratching post

✅5.🧼Grooming🧼

    Tabby cats usually have short fur

  • Routine:
  • Brush once a week
  • Trim nails every 3–4 weeks
  • Check ears and eyes occasionally

✅6.🩺Health Care

    Important things to check with a vet:

  • Vaccination up to date
  • Deworming
  • Flea/tick prevention
  • Spaying/neutering (usually around 6-9 months)

✅7.Bonding With Your Cat

  • Speak softly
  • Offer treats occasionally
  • Let the cat come to you first
  • Play daily
